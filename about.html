About Me

Hi! My name is Fran Hamper. I'm a part-time jeweler (and a part-time math teacher) from North Ridgeville, Ohio.

I've been designing jewelry for ten years. My style is delicate and feminine and I particularly like creating designs with appealing and unusual color combinations. I use freshwater pearls, Swarovski crystals, and semi-precious gemstones, as well as, sterling silver and gold-fill.

My jewelry is on display at various craft shows in northern Ohio. See my Craft Show Schedule for a list of my upcoming shows. I also welcome special orders and custom design orders. For these, contact me by email at: fwhamper@thedanglingmodifier.com
